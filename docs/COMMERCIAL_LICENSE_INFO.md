### When to Choose Commercial License:
- ✅ You need immediate deployment
- ✅ Accuracy is critical (sub-arcsecond)

- ✅ Budget is constrained
- ✅ You have 2-3 weeks for development
- ✅ Outer planets not critical
- ✅ You accept slightly lower accuracy
- ✅ You prefer full code ownership

## Questions to Ask Astrodienst

When contacting them, ask these specific questions:

1. **Pricing:**
   - What is the one-time license fee?
   - Is there an annual renewal cost?
   - Are there volume discounts?

2. **Usage Rights:**
   - Can I use it in a SaaS product?
   - Can I distribute compiled binaries?
   - Do my customers need a license?

3. **Technical:**
   - Do you provide a non-GPL npm package?
   - Is WASM version available with commercial license?
   - What is the update/support policy?

4. **Legal:**
   - Can I keep my code proprietary?
   - Do I need to mention Swiss Ephemeris in credits?
   - What are the redistribution restrictions?
